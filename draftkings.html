There are many different contest types made available through Draft Kings. They include Head to Head matches, Small buy-in non-guaranteed contests, contests that half the player field gets paid and many others. There are double-ups, triple-ups, qualifier events which will allow players to bet a small contest to win entry to a bigger contest.

You are also able to make you own contest in any sport available. You control when the contest takes place, the type of contest it will be, and what the entry fee will be. You are also given the choice to have a public contest, or to make it private and invite friends.

Draft Kings allows for rather large prize pools. During NFL season DK runs tournaments with $1 million guaranteed prize pools. Other sports range in the six figures prize pools, with many options for those who play with a limited bankroll ($1-$2 contests).
